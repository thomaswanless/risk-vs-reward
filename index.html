RULES

How to play:

1. Roll between 1-4 dice each turn.


2. If you roll a 1 on any dice, you lose a life and do NOT get to add your dice roll to your score.


3. Keep rolling in turns, the more dice you roll the greater the chance of scoring lots of points but also the more chance of rolling a 1.


4. When either player runs out of lives, all rolling stops. The player with lives left gets a bonus 50 points before the final judging!
